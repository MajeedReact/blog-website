Check out the new 2024 Mustang!

Published: September 23 2023, Author: Abdulmajeed

Street Legal. Track Ready.

Hear the roar of the all-new 2024 Mustang® Dark Horse™ model as it drowns out all distractions. It features the highest horsepower 5.0L Coyote™ V8 engine ever found in the Mustang® lineup.
Adrenaline Boosting Drift Brake Amp up the fun and exhilaration at every turn on the track with the available, easy-to-use, class-first* Drift Brake. *Class is Sports Cars. Closed track. Drift brake is intended for track use only. It should not be used on public roads. See Owner’s Manual for information on use.

What’s New in the 2024 Ford Mustang?

With sales starting in the summer of 2023, the Mustang will reach its seventh generation. The new look gives the Mustang some much-needed improvements. There is a new lineup of options for people who genuinely need speed among its various styling and powertrain upgrades.
A Performance Pack equipment upgrade will be available for the Mustang GT and EcoBoost versions. The Performance Pack includes:

  • A MagneRide active-dampening suspension.
  • Wider rims and tires.
  • Larger Brembo brakes.
  • A Torsen limited-slip differential.
  • A front strut-tower brace.
